How this works

Only approved users registered here can create bookings from the public calendar page.

Adding someone here does not activate them immediately — they receive an email with a button to confirm before the account (or admin access, if assigned) goes live.

If the email does not exist or isn't approved yet, the booking is blocked automatically.
